# Problem Statement

Agricultural resource planning involves the efficient allocation of limited resources such as water, land, and energy to maximize productivity while ensuring sustainability. However, this process is inherently complex due to the presence of uncertainty in environmental conditions, resource availability, and climate variability.

Traditional approaches to agricultural planning often focus on single-objective optimization or assume stable and predictable conditions. Such methods fail to capture the dynamic and uncertain nature of real-world agricultural systems.

As a result, there is a need for a robust and adaptive framework that can simultaneously:
- Handle multiple conflicting objectives
- Incorporate uncertainty into the decision-making process
- Provide sustainable and efficient solutions for agricultural resource allocation
