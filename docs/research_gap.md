# Research Gap

A comprehensive review of existing literature reveals several limitations in current approaches to agricultural resource planning:

## Key Observations

- Most research focuses on multi-objective optimization techniques such as Genetic Algorithms and NSGA-II.
- Several studies incorporate uncertainty using fuzzy logic or probabilistic models.
- However, these aspects are often addressed independently rather than in an integrated manner.

## Identified Gap

- Lack of unified frameworks that combine:
  - Multi-objective optimization
  - Uncertainty modeling

- Existing models:
  - Struggle with scalability
  - Are limited to specific regions or datasets
  - Do not generalize well to real-world conditions

## Conclusion

There is a clear need for an integrated framework that combines evolutionary optimization techniques with uncertainty modeling to enable more effective and sustainable agricultural decision-making.
